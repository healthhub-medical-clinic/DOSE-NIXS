Who We Are

Dose-Nix is Built to Make Healthcare Smarter,Safer,and More Accessible. We Belive that Medicine Awareness and Support Should be Available To Everyone and Our App is the First Step Toward that Mission.

Our Features Empower Users With Support System,Health Tracking Tools,Tools,AL-Powered Checkers.and Emergency Help-Backed by a Compassionate Design and Expert Insights.
